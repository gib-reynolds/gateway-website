Independent Deal Assessment

Gateway Operational Holdings provides disciplined, operator-informed assessment of small business acquisition targets for search fund entrepreneurs and self-funded searchers.

We evaluate deals through the lens of someone who has actually operated the kinds of businesses our clients want to buy. Our four-pillar framework surfaces the risks and opportunities that don't show up in a CIM — so you can make confident decisions before committing capital.

We answer two questions: Should you spend more time on this deal? And what should you ask the seller next?

Four-Pillar Assessment

I

Business Model Viability

Revenue durability, competitive moat, market position, pricing power, scalability potential.

II

Unit Economics & Financial Health

True margins, cash flow quality, cost structure, revenue concentration, debt capacity.

III

Operational Sustainability

Owner dependency, process maturity, team capability, service delivery, growth capacity.

IV

Risk Profile

Regulatory exposure, customer concentration, CapEx requirements, market risk, transition risk.

The Operator's Lens

Gateway's principal brings direct operational experience across multiple blue-collar and service-based businesses. This isn't theoretical — every assessment is filtered through the reality of actually running these kinds of companies.

That operator's perspective surfaces risks that financial analysis alone misses: the crew dynamics, the equipment maintenance cycles, the regulatory compliance burden, the customer relationships that exist only in the owner's phone.
